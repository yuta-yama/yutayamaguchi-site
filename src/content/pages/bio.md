Yuta Yamaguchi is an award-winning director, cinematographer, and editor in Austin, TX. His filmography includes work that has screened at Cannes, Sundance, SXSW, and HBO. Indiewire praised his "striking visual work" on Ya'Ke Smith's *Wolf*, which premiered at SXSW and won the Panavision Best Texas Feature Film Award at the Dallas International Film Festival.

Winner of numerous ADDY awards, Yuta has worked with clients including DJI, Dell, HBO, MTV, McDonald's, and Amazon. He served as Associate Producer on *August Evening*, which won the John Cassavetes Award at the Independent Spirit Awards. Bilingual in English and Japanese, he is a graduate of the Radio-Television-Film program at the University of Texas at Austin.
