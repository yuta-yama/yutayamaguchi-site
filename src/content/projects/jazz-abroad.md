Jazz Abroad: G.G. - A Jazz Trio

Documentary. Director, Producer, Editor.
🎞Watch Online at [ Seed&Spark ] or [ YouTube ] 🎞

A short documentary film about the Jazz trio G.G. (aka Groovin Girls). Members include Takana Miyamoto, Noriko Ueda, and Masumi Jones.

Synopsis: Three female Japanese musicians travel from Japan to the Berklee College of Music in the US to pursue their musical dreams.

Festivals: SXSW Film Festival, Asian Film Festival of Dallas, San Diego Asian Film Festival, Austin Asian American Film Festival, Motion Blue Yokohama Japan
